Planning

We're RIBA Client Advisers and we help clients design the process of delivering projects - especially those that are complex and strategic. Starting from square one, we work with the client's business plan, or financial model to map out what each project, or series of projects are aiming to achieve. We help appoint the right consultants and set up methods of collaborative working with the wide variety of stakeholders involved in such projects.

We have a special expertise in participatory design and we use our own toolkit to allow non-professionals to input meaningfully into the design development. We run architecture competitions, write briefs, hold design reviews and have experience in both public and private sector procurement. Each process builds a body of evidence that supports a planning application and we have led design teams through complex planning processes on numerous types and scale of project.
